Install Kalorat on iPhone

Add the web app to your home screen in four steps. It works just like a native app.

1

Open in Safari

Open the Kalorat web app in Safari. This only works in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the share icon (square with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of Safari.

3

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen."

4

Tap "Add"

Confirm the name and tap Add. Kalorat will appear on your home screen as a standalone app.
